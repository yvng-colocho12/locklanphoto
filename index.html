About

Locklan B. McNeil started out shooting from the chain-link fence at his hometown's Friday night football games, and never really left the sideline.

What began as a hobby with a borrowed lens turned into a habit of showing up early, staying through overtime, and knowing exactly where to stand when the play breaks toward the end zone. Locklan shoots youth leagues, high school programs, and club teams across the region, with an eye for the moments that don't make the box score — the sprint before the catch, the bench reaction, the parents in the stands.

Every gallery is shot to be used: clean backgrounds, sharp focus on the athlete, and fast turnaround so teams have photos before the season ends, not after.
